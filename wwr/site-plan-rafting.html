Overview

Purpose

To welcome new participants to the sport of whitewater rafting. If is your first time and you do not know what to expect, this is a great oportunity to learn about this amazing sport.

Audience

-Who are the target customers? Mainly groups of friends or entire families, also for beginners in the sport or even for tourists. -What do they want? Looking for new experiences and want to spend a fun time together, improve their skills in the sport of rafting and an adventure. -What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The characteristics and requirements for this sport, also about the rafting equipment or the best river for doing it, the price, etc.
